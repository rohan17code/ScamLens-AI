Never share OTP, password, PIN, CVV, or bank details with anyone.
Messages asking for OTP urgently are high risk.
Messages asking for money transfer or processing fees can be scams.
Fake lottery, prize, reward, giveaway, or winner messages are usually scams.
Do not click unknown links claiming prize, reward, account unlock, or KYC update.
Unknown links in normal documents are not always scams; check context before marking risk.
Resumes, academic results, certificates, invoices, and normal documents can contain safe links.
